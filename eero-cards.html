Celebrating eero employee milestones

A series of print cards to welcome eero employees on their first day of work and to celebrate work anniversaries. Each of the cards has the same base design, and I used eero's brand colors and designed numbers & lettering composed of geometric shapes to differentiate between each event. For the anniversary cards, the confetti is positioned in concentric circles around the celebratory message like a wifi signal, which alludes to eero's smart home wifi system. I had a lot of fun creating these designs :)
