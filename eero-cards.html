Celebrating eero employee milestones

A series of print cards to welcome eero employees on their first day of work and to celebrate their work anniversaries. I designed each of the cards to have the same base design and used eero's brand colors and designed numbers & lettering composed of geometric shapes to differentiate between each event. For the anniversary cards, the confetti is positioned in concentric circles around the celebratory message like a wifi signal, alluding to the eero smart home wifi system.
