Bank of the West

Graphic design & Art Direction


For this Bank of the West advertisement, XYZ collaborated with Publicis & Hal Riney to create a bustling port city scene. We wanted the city to include both industrial and agricultural elements, while maintaining a realistic look and feel of a computer chip. I designed the chip patterns and art directed the 3D creation of the chip. Patterns were created using Illustrator.
