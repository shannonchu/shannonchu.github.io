eero

Graphic Design


In early 2019, eero was acquired by Amazon and, as such, adopted Amazon’s company values. eero came to XYZ to help them create a poster of Amazon’s 14 Leaderships Principles for their office spaces. They wanted us to create graphics symbolizing each principle, and for the style to be very literal, using simple shapes and eero’s bright and playful brand colors. I also created a second, more in-depth poster that lists out all the principles and their full descriptions, sans graphics. These are the final results.
