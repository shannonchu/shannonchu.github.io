eero Welcome & Anniversary Cards

Graphic Design


A series of print cards to welcome eero employees on their first day of work and to celebrate their work anniversaries. Each of the cards to have the same base design, and I used eero's brand colors and designed numbers & lettering composed of geometric shapes to differentiate between each event. For the anniversary cards, the confetti is positioned in concentric circles around the celebratory message to look like a wifi signal, alluding to the eero smart home wifi system.
